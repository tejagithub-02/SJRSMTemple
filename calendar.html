Significance of Our Events

At Sri Jayarama Seva Mandali, our events are deeply rooted in the traditions of Sanatana Dharma, celebrating the teachings of great saints like Sri Madhwacharya, Sri Raghavendra Swamy, and Sri Purandara Dasa. Each program is designed to foster spiritual growth, community unity, and devotion.

The Prakarothsava Seva and Samoohika Sri Vishnusahasranama Parayana every Saturday offer devotees a weekly opportunity to connect with the divine through collective chanting and rituals. The Pratishta Vardhanti Mahotsava marks the temple’s founding, while Sri Ramanavami and the Rathotsava bring joyous celebrations of Lord Rama.

Join us to experience the divine atmosphere, participate in sacred rituals, and connect with our vibrant community in Bangalore.
