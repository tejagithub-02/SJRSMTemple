ಸಾಮೂಹಿಕ ಶ್ರೀ ಸತ್ಯನಾರಾಯಣ ಪೂಜ

The ಸಾಮೂಹಿಕ ಶ್ರೀ ಸತ್ಯನಾರಾಯಣ ಪೂಜ is a revered group ritual dedicated to Lord Satyanarayana, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, known for granting truth, prosperity, and fulfillment of desires. Held at Sri Jayarama Seva Mandali, this sacred ceremony brings devotees together to seek blessings for happiness, success, and spiritual growth. The pooja begins with an invocation of Lord Ganesha to ensure a smooth ritual, followed by the recitation of the Satyanarayana Vrat Katha, a series of stories narrating the miracles of Lord Satyanarayana. Devotees offer items such as bananas, ghee, and tulsi leaves, and participate in the aarti and mantra chanting led by experienced priests. The atmosphere is filled with devotion as participants perform sankalpa (intention-setting) to express their prayers and gratitude. This pooja is particularly significant for those seeking divine intervention for family well-being, career advancement, or resolution of personal challenges. The ritual concludes with the distribution of prasadam, typically including sweetened wheat flour and fruits, which devotees partake in with reverence. Join us to experience the divine grace of Lord Satyanarayana, immerse yourself in the spiritual ambiance, and receive blessings for a truthful and prosperous life.
