About Sri Jayarama Seva Mandali

Our Mission

Sri Jayarama Seva Mandali, established in 1965 in Jayanagar, Bangalore, is dedicated to preserving and promoting Indian cultural heritage through music, dance, and the teachings of The Ramayana. We host events, publish literature, and nurture young talent to uphold tradition.

Our Activities

Ramayana Paarayana

Regular recitations and discourses on The Ramayana to spread its timeless values.

Music & Dance

Concerts and performances by renowned artists and young talents during festivals.

Publications

Books like *Srirama Sambhava* and *Srimad Ramayana Chitramanjari* to preserve heritage.

Celebrating 60 Years of Cultural Excellence

Our Humble Beginnings

In the 1960s, South Bengaluru sought venues for cultural expression, and Kannada writer G Venkatasubbiah (GV) spearheaded the vision. The Jayarama Seva Mandali Auditorium in Jayanagar was built in 1965, starting with Ramayana Paarayana and music concerts. Legends like Balamuralikrishna, RK Srikantan, and Veena Doreswamy Iyengar graced its stage, laying the foundation for a legacy of art and tradition.

Our Mission & Legacy

Rooted in spreading the values of The Ramayana, Sri Jayarama Seva Mandali has been a beacon of music, dance, and discourse. With a publications unit producing nearly two dozen titles—including GV’s *Srirama Sambhava* and the *Srimad Ramayana Chitramanjari*—we preserve cultural heritage. Our youth talent series and annual Ramanavami programs continue this mission.

60th Anniversary Highlights

From April 6 to April 21, 2025, we celebrated our diamond jubilee with performances by Ranjani Vasuki, Ravikiran, and Pt Nagaraj Rao Havaldar. Stay tuned for more cultural events this year!
